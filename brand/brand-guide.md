# Sello Bravo — Brand Guide

> **Orgullo con sello propio.** *(Stamped with pride.)*

## What it is

**Sello Bravo** is a Mexican merch brand that turns *orgullo* — regional pride,
professions, family, fiestas, and good humor — into bold, well-made everyday
objects: **tazas, playeras, y prints**. Every piece carries *el sello* — a mark of
authenticity and attitude. Sold on Amazon México and Mercado Libre, print-on-demand
(no inventory).

## The idea behind the name

*Sello* = a **seal/stamp** — and literally the print we put on every product.
*Bravo* = bold and spirited, and **¡bravo!** — applause, pride. The name *is* the
craft: **the bold mark.** It's suggestive (ownable, trademark-able), not descriptive.

## Personality & voice

Proud · warm · bold · a little cheeky. **Elevated-folk, modern-mexican** — never
touristy-kitsch, never the flag-cliché. We celebrate lo nuestro with taste.

**Voice:** Spanish-first, confident, short, witty; MX idioms used with affection.
- ✅ *"Lo nuestro, bien hecho."* · *"Con orgullo y con sello."* · *"Hecho con causa."*
- ❌ corporate filler, over-explaining, sad-beige minimalism, "auténtico mexicano™" clichés.

## Color palette

A warm, craft-premium MX palette — copper, ink, and bone, with controlled pops.
Lead with **Cobre** + **Tinta** on **Hueso**; use Nopal / Ámbar / Rosa as accents only.

| Token | Name | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Hero | **Cobre** (terracotta) | `#C0492C` | logo, primary art, headlines |
| Ink | **Tinta** | `#1B1714` | type, line art, dark print |
| Base | **Hueso** (bone) | `#F3EADB` | backgrounds, natural-product canvas |
| Accent | **Nopal** (deep green) | `#2F5D50` | secondary art, balance |
| Accent | **Ámbar** (gold) | `#E2A53C` | highlights, fiesta warmth |
| Pop | **Rosa Mexicano** | `#D2326B` | sparing energy pop (≤10% of a design) |

## Typography (all free for commercial use — Google Fonts / OFL)

- **Display / wordmark — `Anton`**: bold condensed grotesque. Loud, stamp-friendly.
- **Labels / seal text — `Oswald`**: condensed; small-caps, the seal's curved type.
- **Body / secondary — `Bitter`**: warm slab serif; readable, bilingual.

Pairing rule: **Anton** for the shout, **Bitter** for the sentence, **Oswald** for the label.

## Logo — *El Sello*

The primary mark is a **circular seal/stamp** (see `logo/sello-bravo-seal.svg`):
a double ring, curved `SELLO BRAVO` over a central star and `ORGULLO MEXICANO`.

- **One-color by design** (true stamp feel): default **Cobre**; **Tinta** or **Hueso** (knockout) are the only other approved fills.
- **Clear space:** keep ≥ 15% of the seal's diameter clear on all sides.
- **Min size:** 24 mm / 96 px (seal stays legible).
- **Don't:** recolor outside the three approved fills, add gradients/shadows, stretch, or rotate past ±8°, or place on busy photos.
- **Lockups:** seal-only (favicon, product tag), or seal + `SELLO BRAVO` wordmark (Anton) for headers.

## House style for product designs

Stamp / emblem + bold type. Centered lockups; badge framing; the seal as a recurring
device. Art in **Tinta** or **Cobre** on **white / natural (Hueso)** products; Rosa /
Ámbar / Nopal as accents. Phrases are short, punchy, and proudly local. Keep it
**legible at a glance** (it has to sell as a thumbnail).

## POD production specs (Printful / Printify → Amazon MX / Mercado Libre)

- **Taza (11 oz):** wraparound art area ~**2475 × 1155 px @ 300 DPI** (≈ 20.8 × 9.7 cm); or a centered ~9 × 9 cm panel per side. Export **PNG, transparent**, sRGB.
- **Playera (DTG):** front print up to **~30 × 40 cm @ 300 DPI** (≈ 3600 × 4800 px). Export **PNG, transparent**, sRGB; supply a **light-garment** and a **dark-garment** (knockout to Hueso) version.
- **Prints/posters:** vector (SVG/PDF) or 300 DPI PNG at target size.
- **Color:** design in the palette above; expect ~5–8% color shift in DTG — avoid relying on Rosa for fine text.
- **Files:** keep editable source per design; hand POD the flattened transparent PNG at the spec size.
