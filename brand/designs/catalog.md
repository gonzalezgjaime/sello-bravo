# Sello Bravo — Launch Design Batch (v1)

Eight print-ready concepts across the highest-signal MX niches (mapped to the
analyzer's `niches.json` + seasonal peaks). House style: **stamp/emblem + bold type**,
art in **Tinta `#1B1714`** or **Cobre `#C0492C`** on white/natural products, accents
sparing. Each is sized per `../brand-guide.md` → POD specs. Hand the flattened
transparent PNG to Printful/Printify; keep editable source.

Legend: 🟢 = evergreen · 📅 = seasonal peak (lean into it on that calendar)

---

### 1 · Orgullo Mexicano — *playera* (hero brand tee) 🟢
- **Lockup:** `ORGULLO` / `MEXICANO` stacked in Anton, the **Sello** seal centered above, a single star dot between words.
- **Copy alt:** "ORGULLO SIN PEDIR PERMISO" · "MÉXICO, MI SELLO".
- **Color:** Tinta art on natural; Cobre seal. Dark-garment variant = Hueso knockout.
- **Why:** the flagship — wears the brand itself; the everyday-pride buyer.

### 2 · Tapatío de corazón — *taza* 📅 (Guadalajara/Jalisco pride; year-round, spikes Sept fiestas)
- **Lockup:** `TAPATÍO` big (Anton), `DE CORAZÓN` script-ish below, small cantera-arch motif + seal.
- **Copy alt:** "PURO GUADALAJARA" · "DE JALISCO Y QUÉ".
- **Color:** Cobre + Tinta on white. Ámbar accent on the arch.
- **Niche:** `taza-guadalajara`.

### 3 · Confía, soy ingeniero — *taza* 🟢 (profession humor)
- **Lockup:** `CONFÍA —` / `SOY INGENIERO` in Anton; a tiny gear + escuadra (set-square) as the seal's center motif.
- **Copy alt:** "INGENIERO: donde 'ahí se va' no existe" · "EL PLANO SOY YO".
- **Color:** Tinta on white; Cobre gear.
- **Niche:** `taza-ingeniero`. *(Swap the profession to spin variants: enfermera, maestra, contador, arquitecto.)*

### 4 · Mamá: jefa, reina y todo — *taza* 📅 (Día de las Madres, 10 May — peak Apr–May)
- **Lockup:** `MAMÁ` huge (Anton), `JEFA · REINA · Y TODO` in Oswald small-caps under a flor/corona emblem + seal.
- **Copy alt:** "LA MEJOR MAMÁ, con sello" · "MAMÁ NIVEL: LEYENDA".
- **Color:** Cobre + Rosa Mexicano pop on the flor; Tinta type.
- **Niche:** `taza-dia-madres`.

### 5 · Los nuestros nunca se van — *playera* 📅 (Día de Muertos, late Oct–Nov)
- **Lockup:** a tasteful **calaca + cempasúchil** emblem (stamp-style, line art), `LOS NUESTROS` / `NUNCA SE VAN` in Anton below.
- **Copy alt:** "VIVOS EN LA MEMORIA" · "CON FLORES LOS RECORDAMOS".
- **Color:** Tinta line art on natural; Ámbar + Cobre marigold accents. Dark variant = Hueso/Ámbar.
- **Niche:** `playera-dia-muertos`. Keep it **reverent, not spooky-kitsch**.

### 6 · Enfermera: corazón de acero — *taza* 🟢📅 (gift; Día de la Enfermera 6 Jan)
- **Lockup:** `ENFERMERA` (Anton), `CORAZÓN DE ACERO · MANOS DE ÁNGEL` in Oswald; a heart + cruz emblem in the seal.
- **Color:** Cobre heart, Tinta type on white.
- **Niche:** `taza-enfermera`. *(Same template → doctor, dentista, paramédico.)*

### 7 · Mis XV, con sello — *playera* (+ taza) 📅 (quinceañera season, Mar–Jul)
- **Lockup:** personalizable — `[NOMBRE]` script + `MIS XV` in Anton + date, framed by the seal as a keepsake "stamp."
- **Copy alt:** "XV AÑOS · [NOMBRE] · [FECHA]" · party-favor set (12-pack tee/taza).
- **Color:** Rosa Mexicano + Ámbar on Hueso; Tinta names. Elegant, not cutesy.
- **Niche:** `playera-quinceanera`. **Made-to-order personalization = premium price + bundles.**

### 8 · Maestra: siembra futuros — *taza* 📅 (Día del Maestro, 15 May)
- **Lockup:** `MAESTRA` (Anton), `SIEMBRA FUTUROS` in Oswald; a small planta/libro emblem in the seal.
- **Copy alt:** "GRACIAS, MAESTRA" · "FORMA MENTES, DEJA HUELLA".
- **Color:** Nopal + Tinta on white.
- **Niche:** `taza-maestra`. *(Template → maestro, educadora.)*
